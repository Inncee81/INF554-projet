Welcome to Isara


An engineering school specialised in agriculture, agribusiness and
environment

   Isara offers a wide range of Bachelor and Master level curricula in
   agriculture, food science, social science, agribusiness and
   environmental science. We also develop research activities and advisory
   services, mainly in two fields.

Our two fields of expertise

     * INNOVATION &
       ENTREPRENEURSHIP
     * AGROECOLOGY &
       SUSTAINABLE FOOD SYSTEMS
