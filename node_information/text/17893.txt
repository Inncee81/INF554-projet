Scan your information system for vulnerabilities

Scan’ER lets you scan your networks and information systems to find any
computers open to outside access, detect any vulnerabilities on them and
produce a general security analysis of your network.


   The Scan’ER interface enables you to do the following:
     * Run regular or immediate scans on computers or groups of computers
       that you specify.
     * Obtain comprehensive information (consequences, resolution, etc.)
       on any vulnerabilities discovered.
     * Extract summary or detailed reports in PDF or CSV format.
     * Display a security dashboard for your network based on ratings
       given to the computers.


   Find out more!

   Included in the RENATER package.

   Use of this service is reserved for Chief Information Security
   Officers or the Security Correspondent named in PASS.
     __________________________________________________________________

   Souscribe in portail PASS.

   Accessible via : scaner.renater.fr

   TCS par RENATER

Electronic certificates for your applications

TCS enables you to obtain electronic certificates for securing your online
applications.


   RENATER offers its community electronic certificates that are
   recognised by most software by default (web browsers, e-mail clients,
   smartphones, etc.). These certificates do not require any configuration
   on the client workstations.

   These services are the French version of the contract signed between
   the GéANT Association and the Digicert commercial certification
   provider for the benefit of around twenty national higher education and
   research networks in Europe, including RENATER.

   Four services are available:
     * A Server Certificates service.
     * A Personal Certificates service.
     * An Object Signature Certificates service.
     * A Document Signature Certificates service.


   Find out more!

   Included in the RENATER package.
     __________________________________________________________________

   Souscribe in portail PASS.


   Accessed via:
     * digicert.com/secure
       by the named administrators and
     * digicert.com/sso by users

Scan your information system for vulnerabilities

Scan’ER lets you scan your networks and information systems to find any
computers open to outside access, detect any vulnerabilities on them and
produce a general security analysis of your network.


   The Scan’ER interface enables you to do the following:
     * Run regular or immediate scans on computers or groups of computers
       that you specify.
     * Obtain comprehensive information (consequences, resolution, etc.)
       on any vulnerabilities discovered.
     * Extract summary or detailed reports in PDF or CSV format.
     * Display a security dashboard for your network based on ratings
       given to the computers.


   Find out more!

   Included in the RENATER package.

   Use of this service is reserved for Chief Information Security
   Officers or the Security Correspondent named in PASS.

   TCS par RENATER

Electronic certificates for your applications

TCS enables you to obtain electronic certificates for securing your online
applications.


   RENATER offers its community electronic certificates that are
   recognised by most software by default (web browsers, e-mail clients,
   smartphones, etc.). These certificates do not require any configuration
   on the client workstations.

   These services are the French version of the contract signed between
   the GéANT Association and the Digicert commercial certification
   provider for the benefit of around twenty national higher education and
   research networks in Europe, including RENATER.

   Four services are available:
     * A Server Certificates service.
     * A Personal Certificates service.
     * An Object Signature Certificates service.
     * A Document Signature Certificates service.


   Find out more!

   Included in the RENATER package.
     __________________________________________________________________

   Souscribe in portail PASS.


   Accessed via:
     * digicert.com/secure
       by the named administrators and
     * digicert.com/sso by users
