Exchange Year / Semester

   International students selected by our partner institutions abroad have
   the opportunity to spend one semester or one year of their studies at
   Centrale Nantes. They usually join the final year of the engineering
   programme choosing both a specialisation and a professional option to
   obtain ECTS credits. A selection of Master courses are also proposed
   within this framework. Course are available in English and in French.
     * Click here to apply and see course list

Exchange Year / Semester

   International students selected by our partner institutions abroad have
   the opportunity to spend one semester or one year of their studies at
   Centrale Nantes. They usually join the final year of the engineering
   programme choosing both a specialisation and a professional option to
   obtain ECTS credits. A selection of Master courses are also proposed
   within this framework. Course are available in English and in French.

Research Project

   Students from a partner institutions wishing to undertake a research
   project at Centrale Nantes:
   IMPORTANT: Before contacting the International Office
   (international@ec-nantes.fr), you must have already contacted and
   secured agreement for your project from the Centrale Nantes professor
   in charge of the discipline you are interested in. Without this prior
   agreement we are not able to accept your registration.
   Once this is done, please send us an email with all the details
   discussed with your teacher and we'll explain you how to finalise your
   registration.
   Teaching staff to contact:

   Civil Engineering Syed Yasir ALAM

   Digital City Myriam SERVIERES

Research Project

   Students from a partner institutions wishing to undertake a research
   project at Centrale Nantes:
   IMPORTANT: Before contacting the International Office
   (international@ec-nantes.fr), you must have already contacted and
   secured agreement for your project from the Centrale Nantes professor
   in charge of the discipline you are interested in. Without this prior
   agreement we are not able to accept your registration.
   Once this is done, please send us an email with all the details
   discussed with your teacher and we'll explain you how to finalise your
   registration.
   Teaching staff to contact:

Exchange Year / Semester

   International students selected by our partner institutions abroad have
   the opportunity to spend one semester or one year of their studies at
   Centrale Nantes. They usually join the final year of the engineering
   programme choosing both a specialisation and a professional option to
   obtain ECTS credits. A selection of Master courses are also proposed
   within this framework. Course are available in English and in French.
     * Click here to apply and see course list

Exchange Year / Semester

   International students selected by our partner institutions abroad have
   the opportunity to spend one semester or one year of their studies at
   Centrale Nantes. They usually join the final year of the engineering
   programme choosing both a specialisation and a professional option to
   obtain ECTS credits. A selection of Master courses are also proposed
   within this framework. Course are available in English and in French.
     * Click here to apply and see course list

Research Project

   Students from a partner institutions wishing to undertake a research
   project at Centrale Nantes:
   IMPORTANT: Before contacting the International Office
   (international@ec-nantes.fr), you must have already contacted and
   secured agreement for your project from the Centrale Nantes professor
   in charge of the discipline you are interested in. Without this prior
   agreement we are not able to accept your registration.
   Once this is done, please send us an email with all the details
   discussed with your teacher and we'll explain you how to finalise your
   registration.
   Teaching staff to contact:

   Discipline Contact
   Aeronautics Guy CAPDEVILLE
   Civil Engineering Syed Yasir ALAM
   Computer Science Jean-Yves MARTIN
