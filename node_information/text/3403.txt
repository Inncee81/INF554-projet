iCloud

The best place for all your photos, files, and more.

   iCloud is built into every Apple device. That means all your stuff —
   photos, files, notes, and more — is safe, up to date, and available
   wherever you are. And it works automatically, so all you have to do is
   keep doing what you love. Everyone gets 5GB of free iCloud storage to
   start, and it’s easy to add more at any time.

iCloud Photos

All your photos and videos.
Always available.

A lifetime of photos
on all your devices.

   With iCloud Photos, you can browse, search, and share all the photos
   and videos from any of your devices, whether they were taken yesterday
   or years ago.

Take all the photos you want. Without worrying about space on your devices.

   To save space on your devices, the original, full‑resolution photos you
   take are automatically uploaded to iCloud. You’ll still always have a
   lightweight version of every photo on your device, and you can download
   the originals whenever you need them.

The easiest way to share photos with friends and family.

   Shared Albums lets you create albums for trips, events, or whatever you
   want. Simply choose the friends and family members who can see,
   comment, and add their own photos and videos.

iCloud Drive

Any type of file.
All in one place.

All your files available from all your devices.

   The files you keep safe in iCloud Drive are easy to get to, no matter
   which device you’re using. You can access all your files from the Files
   app on iOS, the Finder on your Mac, File Explorer for Windows PCs,
   or iCloud.com.

Organize files however you want. Get to them on any device you want.

   iCloud Drive lets you organize your files with folders, rename them,
   and tag them with colors. And when you make a change, the update is
   made across all your devices. You can even have everything in your Mac
   Desktop and Documents folders automatically available in iCloud Drive.

Work together. Simply by sending a link.

   With iCloud Drive it’s easy to invite people to work on a file with
   you. Just send a private link. No creating copies, managing versions,
   or adding email attachments.

Apps using iCloud

Your favorite apps are
even better with iCloud.

All your essentials.
Accessible from all your devices.

   iCloud automatically keeps apps — including Mail, Calendar, Contacts,
   Reminders, Safari, and some of your favorite apps from the App Store —
   updated across all your devices. So when you add a new phone number,
   bookmark a website, or update a slide in a presentation, the change
   appears everywhere.

Collaborate with Pages, Numbers, Keynote, and Notes.

   Work with others on documents, presentations, and spreadsheets and see
   edits as they’re made. With live updates on all your devices, your
   collaborators will always be on the same page.

Every message. Updated across all your devices.

   iCloud now automatically stores all of your messages. Search for an
   address, a funny exchange, or anything else. And you can pick up every
   conversation right where you left off. Even when you move to a new
   phone or computer.

iCloud Backup and Restore

All the things that matter.
Safe and secure.

Automatic backups give you peace of mind.

   iCloud automatically backs up your iOS devices when they’re connected
   to power and Wi-Fi. So if you lose your device or get a new one, you’ll
   have all the things that matter without missing a beat.^1

Move to a new device with everything you loved about your old one.

   iCloud makes moving your settings, photos, apps, and documents to a new
   device seamless. Just sign in to iCloud when you set up your new
   device, and you’ll be ready to go in minutes.

Two‑factor authentication helps keep your data safe.

   Two‑factor authentication is an extra layer of security designed to

   or Mac.

Find the storage plan that’s right for you.

iCloud

The best place for all your photos, files, and more.

   iCloud is built into every Apple device and keeps your stuff safe, up
   to date, and available wherever you are. And it works automatically, so
   all you have to do is keep doing what you love. Everyone gets 5GB of
   free iCloud storage to start, and it’s easy to add more at any time.

iCloud Photo Library

All your photos and videos.
Always available.

A lifetime of photos
on all your devices.

   With iCloud Photo Library, you can browse, search, and share all the
   photos and videos from any of your devices, whether they were taken
   yesterday or years ago.

Take all the photos you want. Without worrying about space on your devices.

   To save space on your devices, the original, full‑resolution photos you
   take are automatically uploaded to iCloud. You’ll still always have a
   lightweight version of every photo on your device, and you can download
   the originals whenever you need them.

The easiest way to share photos with friends and family.

   iCloud Photo Sharing lets you create albums for trips, events, or
   whatever you want. Simply choose the friends and family members who can
   see, comment, and add their own photos and videos.

iCloud Drive

Any type of file.
All in one place.

All your files available from all your devices.

   The files you keep safe in iCloud Drive are easy to get to, no matter
   which device you’re using. You can access all your files from the Files
   app on iOS, the Finder on your Mac, File Explorer for Windows PCs, or
   iCloud.com.

Organize files however you want. Get to them on any device you want.

   iCloud Drive lets you organize your files with folders, rename them,
   and tag them with colors. And when you make a change, the update is
   made across all your devices. You can even have everything in your Mac
   Desktop and Documents folders automatically available in iCloud Drive.

Work together. Simply by sending a link.

   With iCloud Drive it’s easy to invite people to work on a file with
   you. Just send a private link. No creating copies, managing versions,
   or adding email attachments.

Apps using iCloud

Your favorite apps are
even better with iCloud.

All your essentials.
Accessible from all your devices.

   iCloud automatically keeps apps — including Mail, Calendar, Contacts,
   Reminders, Safari, and some of your favorite apps from the App Store —
   updated across all your devices. So when you add a new phone number,
   bookmark a website, or update a slide in a presentation, the change
   appears everywhere.

Collaborate with Pages, Numbers, Keynote, and Notes.

   Work with others on documents, presentations, and spreadsheets and see
   edits as they’re made. With live updates on all your devices, your
   collaborators will always be on the same page.

Every message. Updated across all your devices.

   iCloud now automatically stores all of your messages. Search for an
   address, a funny exchange, or anything else. And you can pick up every
   conversation right where you left off. Even when you move to a new
   phone or computer.

iCloud Backup and Restore

All the things that matter.
Safe and secure.

Automatic backups give you peace of mind.

   iCloud automatically backs up your iOS devices when they’re connected
   to power and Wi-Fi. So if you lose your device or get a new one, you’ll
   have all the things that matter without missing a beat.^1

Move to a new device with everything you loved about your old one.

   iCloud makes moving your settings, photos, apps, and documents to a new
   device seamless. Just sign in to iCloud when you set up your new
   device, and you’ll be ready to go in minutes.

Two‑factor authentication helps keep your data safe.

   Two‑factor authentication is an extra layer of security designed to
   ensure that only you can access your account. That means your account
   can only be accessed on devices you trust, like your iPhone, iPad,
   or Mac.

   Learn more about two‑factor authentication

Find the storage plan that’s right for you.

   The right amount of storage depends on how you and your family members
   want to use your devices. Everyone gets 5GB of free iCloud storage to
   get started and it’s easy to upgrade at any time. Your apps and iTunes
   purchases don’t count toward your iCloud storage space, so you need it
   just for things like photos, videos, files, and device backups.

iCloud

Najlepšie miesto pre všetky tvoje fotky, súbory a oveľa viac.

   iCloud je súčasťou každého zariadenia Apple. Znamená to, že celý tvoj
   obsah – fotky, súbory, poznámky a ďalšie – je
   v bezpečí, aktualizovaný a k dispozícii, nech už si kdekoľvek.
   A funguje to automaticky, takže môžeš robiť len to, čo ťa baví.
   Na začiatok dostaneš v iCloude 5 GB bezplatného úložiska a môžeš si ho
   kedykoľvek jednoducho rozšíriť.

iCloud Knižnica fotiek

Všetky tvoje fotky a videá.
Vždy po ruke.

Celoživotná zbierka fotiek.
Vo všetkých tvojich zariadeniach.

   S iCloud Knižnicou fotiek môžeš prezerať, vyhľadať a zdieľať všetky
   fotky a videá z akéhokoľvek svojho zariadenia. Nech boli vytvorené
   včera alebo pred rokmi.

Odfoť všetko, čo len chceš. O miesto na svojich zariadeniach sa nestaraj.

   Originálne fotky v úplnom rozlíšení sa automaticky nahrávajú
   do iCloudu. Ty tak ušetríš miesto vo svojom zariadení, kde ti zostane
   odľahčená verzia každej fotky. A keď budeš potrebovať originály,
   jednoducho si ich stiahneš.

Najjednoduchší spôsob ako zdieľať fotky s priateľmi a rodinou.

   iCloud Zdieľanie fotiek ti umožňuje vytvárať albumy z ciest, osláv
   alebo čoho len chceš. Stačí iba vybrať priateľov a členov rodiny, ktorí
   ich môžu vidieť, komentovať a pridávať k nim svoje vlastné fotky
   a videá.

iCloud Drive

Všetky typy súborov.
A všetky na jednom mieste.

Všetky svoje súbory máš vo všetkých zariadeniach.

   K svojim súborom bezpečne uloženým v iCloude sa jednoducho dostaneš
   z každého svojho zariadenia. V iOS ich nájdeš cez aplikáciu Súbory,
   v Macu cez Finder, v PC so systémom Windows cez Prieskumníka alebo
   cez webstránku iCloud.com.

Usporadúvaj si súbory podľa seba. A pristupuj k nim z ktoréhokoľvek
zariadenia.

   V iCloud Drive vieš súbory usporiadať do priečinkov, premenovať ich
   alebo farebne označiť. A keď niečo zmeníš, aktualizuje sa to
   vo všetkých tvojich zariadeniach. Dokonca môžeš mať v iCloud Drive
   automaticky k dispozícii aj všetko, čo máš vo svojom Macu na Ploche
   a v priečinku Dokumenty.

Pracujte spoločne. Stačí len poslať link.

   V iCloud Drive vieš jednoducho prizvať ďalšie osoby, aby s tebou
   spolupracovali na nejakom súbore. Stačí im len poslať súkromný link.
   Už žiadne vytváranie kópií, spravovanie verzií ani pridávanie príloh
   do e-mailov.

Aplikácie používajú iCloud

Tvoje obľúbené aplikácie sú
s iCloudom ešte lepšie.

Všetky tvoje najdôležitejšie aplikácie.
Dostupné zo všetkých tvojich zariadení.

   iCloud automaticky aktualizuje aplikácie ako Mail, Kalendár, Kontakty,
   Pripomienky, Safari a niektoré obľúbené aplikácie z App Store
   vo všetkých tvojich zariadeniach. Takže keď si zapíšeš nové telefónne
   číslo, označíš záložkou webstránku alebo aktualizuješ slajd
   v prezentácii, táto zmena sa prejaví všade.

Spolupracuj s ostatnými v Pages, Numbers, Keynotes a Poznámkach.

   Pri spolupráci na dokumentoch, prezentáciách a tabuľkách okamžite
   uvidíš každú zmenu, ktorú niekto urobí. Vďaka nepretržitej
   synchronizácii všetkých tvojich zariadení budú tvoji spolupracovníci
   neustále v obraze.

Všetky tvoje správy. Aktualizované na všetkých tvojich zariadeniach.

   Všetky tvoje správy sa teraz automaticky ukladajú v iCloude. Môžeš
   v nich vyhľadávať adresy, konverzácie alebo čokoľvek iné. A v každej
   konverzácii môžeš pokračovať presne tam, kde si skončil. Aj keď prejdeš
   na nový telefón alebo počítač.

iCloud Zálohovanie a obnovenie

Všetko, na čom ti záleží.
V bezpečí a chránené.

Automatické zálohy ti prinesú pokoj v duši.

   Keď máš svoje zariadenie s iOS pripojené k napájaniu a Wi‑Fi, iCloud ho
   automaticky zálohuje. Ak teda stratíš svoje zariadenie alebo si
   obstaráš nové, budeš mať všetko k dispozícii a o nič neprídeš.^1

Prejdi na nové zariadenie so všetkým, čo miluješ na tom starom.

   iCloud zaisťuje bezproblémový prenos nastavení, fotiek, aplikácií
   a dokumentov do nového zariadenia. Stačí sa pri nastavovaní nového
   zariadenia prihlásiť do iCloudu a po pár minútach to máš vybavené.

Dvojstupňové overovanie pomáha chrániť tvoje dáta.

   Dvojstupňové overovanie predstavuje ďalšiu vrstvu zabezpečenia, ktorá
   má zaistiť, aby prístup k tvojmu kontu nemohol získať nikto iný okrem
   teba. To znamená, že tvoje konto je prístupné len zo zariadení, ktorým
   dôveruješ, ako je tvoj iPhone, iPad alebo Mac.

   Zisti viac o dvojstupňovom overovaní

   tvojej rodiny chcete používať svoje zariadenia. Na začiatku dostaneš

   rozšíriť. Aplikácie a nákupy z iTunes sa do tvojho úložného priestoru
   v iCloude nezapočítavajú. Potrebuješ ho len na obsah, ako sú fotky,
   videá, súbory a zálohy zariadení.
