Culture space: develop yourself!
     __________________________________________________________________

   After classes at the University of Limoges, student life is full of
   opportunities: a multitude of events and cultural activities animate
   the evenings on campus and allow you to meet a variety of people. With
   the Culture Pass, students can attend the artistic and cultural
   workshops, attend many shows and discover French culture.

   The artistic workshops: choir, orchestra, graphic arts, big band,
   circus, theatre, zumba, contemporary dance, writing, arts in health,
   sound design, awakening to sounds and resonance of vegetable fibres,
   introduction to musical and mediaeval paleography and introduction to
   early polyphony. The university’s cultural department also puts on
   cultural events throughout the year. It publishes a cultural guide and
   a calendar.

Espacio cultural: ¡participa!
     __________________________________________________________________

   Además de los cursos universitarios, la vida estudiantil en la
   Universidad de Limoges ofrece muchas oportunidades, multitud de
   manifestaciones y actividades culturales que animan el campus y las
   tardes te permitirán conocer mucha gente. Con el Pass Culture, los
   estudiantes pueden frecuentar talleres artísticos y culturales, asistir
   a numerosos espectáculos, hacer actividades juntos y descubrir la
   cultura francesa.

   Talleres artísticos: coro, orquestra, artes plásticas, big band, circo,
   teatro, zumba, danza contemporánea, escritura, arte sonoro, creación
   sonora, enseñanza de sonidos y resonancia de fibras vegetales,
   iniciación a la paleografía musical e iniciación a la polifonía. El
   servicio de cultura de la universidad también programa eventos
   culturales durante todo el año que puedes consultar en una guía y una
   agenda cultural que publican.

   Contacto:
   Servicio de cultura – Nadine Cogné
   (0033) 672 91 53 94 or (0033) 555 14 91 33

International Welcome Office
     __________________________________________________________________

   Join the University of Limoges to enjoy a personalised and efficient
   welcome. The International Welcome Office caters to all foreign
   nationals hosted at the University of Limoges.

   The International Welcome Office will help you to find accommodation,
   complete administrative paperwork, learn French and discover
   Limoges and its region.

The International Welcome Office, a personalised welcome!

   The International Welcome Office will help you obtain information prior
   to your arrival and will guide you throughout your stay in order to
   perform all the steps required for the success of your induction to
   Limoges.

   The International Welcome Office is a multilingual one-stop shop where
   you can obtain all the information required to make your stay as smooth
   as possible.

An activity programme of discovery and integration

     * International Welcome Day
       Each year, there are some 500 new international students ; about
       200 of them participate in this day of integration which allows
       them to discover our local heritage, but also the strategically
       useful points for their life in Limoges, by way of a treasure hunt.
     * Introduction to Skiing
       The team at the International Welcome Office has, for the last 3
       years, offered its international students a wonderful opportunity
       by proposing that they participate in a day of introduction to
       skiing completely free of charge.

     * Canoeing/kayaking on Vassivière Lake
       The International Welcome Office invites you to discover Vassivière
       Lake, at your own pace and completely freely ! Indulge in a canoe
       adventure to discover the fauna and flora.
     * Mobility hours
       An opportunity for all mobile students to meet and exchange
       experiences.
