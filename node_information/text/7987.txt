Investments director shifts focus to developing climate solutions

Innovation

   Dean Takahashi, the longtime senior director of the Yale Investments
   Office, will spearhead a new multidisciplinary Yale laboratory that
   will develop and support innovative solutions to the challenge of
   climate change.

The Yale Program on Climate Change Communication conducts scientific research
on public climate change knowledge, attitudes, policy preferences, and
behavior, and the underlying psychological, cultural, and political factors
that influence them.

Research

Task force to probe how quickly Yale can achieve net zero carbon emissions

Campus & community

   President Peter Salovey has formed a task force to review and propose
   new ambitious and transformative goals for Yale’s Climate Action
   initiative.
