Follow Liker is an optimized and automated versions of Twitter, Instagram,
Pinterest and Tumblr that you can run 24 hours a day. It is very easy to
setup and use. See some of the benefits, features and what you can do with
FollowLiker:

Follow Users

   Auto-follow users; follow: a user's followers/following, users
   discussing a topic (by keywords/hashtags), by location/language, custom
   list of users (your own list); Follow back your followers; Follow by
   follower to following ratio...

Unfollow Users

   Auto-Unfollow users, unfollow users not following back after some days,
   limit unfollow per run or day, white list users that should not be
   unfollowed, unfollow by follower to following ratio...

Post Tweets

   Keep your Twitter profiles active, engaging and interesting. Spread
   your tweets throughout the day to keep you constantly engaged on
   Twitter. Auto-post tweets; Post tweets from RSS or URLs; Make links in
   tweets unique...

Retweet

   Auto-retweet tweets; Retweet a user's tweets; Retweet tweets posted
   from a specific location; Retweet by language...

Reply Tweets

   Auto-reply tweets; Reply a user's tweets; Reply tweets posted from a
   specific location; Reply tweets by language; Wait and reply...

Favorite Tweets

   Auto-favorite tweets; Favorite a user's tweets; Favorite tweets posted
   from a specific location; Favorite by language...

Send Direct Messages

   Communicate with your followers directly and keep them informed.
   Automatically send direct messages to your followers, send thank you
   messages to new followers... Send Instagram Direct Messages to any user
   or follower...

Share Photos and Videos

   Keep your Instagram profiles active, engaging and interesting.
   Automatically share or upload photos and videos to your Instagram
   accounts directly from your computer; Schedule photo and video
   sharing/uploading; Limit photo and video sharing/uploading...

Like Photos

   Auto-like photos; Like photos posted by a user; Like custom list of
   photos (your own list)...

Unlike Photos

   Auto-unlike photo, unlike photos liked after some days, set unlike
   photo limit with each run, delay unlike to look more natural...

Comment on Photos

   Auto-comment on photos; Comment on a user's photos; Comment on custom
   list of photos (your own list)...

User Search

   Auto-find users to follow based on keywords, other user's
   followers/following, location, interest, current trends, location,
   distance and language. It has the ability to remove results of users
   that only have a default profile picture...

Tweet Search

   Auto-find tweets to be replied, favorited and retweeted by keyword,
   other user's tweets, hashtags, recency, location, distance and
   language. It has the ability to remove tweets that have link, # or @
   symbols...

Photo Search

   Auto-find photos to be liked and commented based on keyword/hashtag
   choices, other user's photos...

Crawl Tweets

   Automatically aggregate real-time website contents and RSS feeds as
   tweets and tweet them to your accounts automatically...

Unfavorite Tweets

   Auto-unfavorite tweets, set unfavorite tweet limit with each run, delay
   unfavorite to look more natural...

Share Pins

   Keep your Pinterest profiles active, engaging and interesting.
   Automatically share pins/photos to your Pinterest accounts directly
   from your computer; Schedule pin sharing; Limit pin sharing...

Repin

   Auto-repin photos; Repin a user's pins...

Like Pins

   Auto-like pins; Like pins shared by a user...

Unlike Pins

   Auto-unlike pin, set unlike pin limit with each run, delay unlike to
   look more natural...

Comment on Pins

   Auto-comment on pins; Comment on a user's pins...

Pin Search

   Auto-find pins to be liked, repinned or commented based on keyword
   choices, other user's pins...

Post Blogs

   Keep your Tumblr profiles active, engaging and interesting.
   Automatically post contents to your Tumblr accounts; Schedule blog
   posting; Limit blog posting...

Like Blogs

   Auto-like blogs; Schedule blog liking...

Unlike Blogs

   Auto-unlike blogs, set unlike blog limit with each run, delay unlike to
   look more natural...

Reblog

   Auto-reblog posts; Reblog text, video, audio... posts, Schedule
   rebogging...

Blog Search

   Auto-find blogs to be liked or reblogged based on keyword choices...

Blacklist Users

   Build a list of users who should not be followed, users whose tweet
   should not be retweeted or replied, users whose photo/media should not
   be liked or commented on. Auto-blacklist unfollowed users...

White-list Users

   Build a special list of users who should not be unfollowed...

Assign Proxies

   Use public or private proxies with each of your accounts as you deem
   fit...

Run Multiple Accounts

Schedule Tasks

   Schedule and automate single or multiple tasks continuously with ease,
   set different schedule time range for each account, set intervals
   between each schedule...

View Account Details

   View current number of followers, tweets, photos, media and followings
   of each account...
