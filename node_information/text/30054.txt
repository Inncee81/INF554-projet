About Cookies Policy

Types of cookies used by the Cartooning for Peace association website and
their purpose

   Some of the features of this site (content sharing on social networks)
   are based on services provided by third-party sites. These features
   deposit cookies that allow these sites to track your navigation. These
   cookies are only stored if you give your consent.

   You can inquire about the nature of the cookies placed, accept or
   refuse them either globally for the entire site and all services, or
   service by service.

Functionnal Cookies:

     * are essential to the proper functioning of this website;
     * allow you, for example, to display the window for requesting
       consent to cookies.


   Name of the cookie Purpose
   _cfduid (addtoany.com) Authentication of site traffic
    tartaucitron Cookie to manage cookies during your navigation on the
   site

Site performance Cookies:

     * allow us to optimize the operation of this website;
     * allow us to collect anonymous information about your visits to this
       site;
     * your IP address is anonymized before it is collected and processed
       by the third party site, Google Analytics.


   Name of the cookie Purpose
   _ga                Google Analytics Statistics
   _gat               Google Analytics Statistics
   _gid               Google Analytics Statistics
   collect            Sending statistics to Google Analytics

Social Media Cookies:

     * allow you to share the content of our website on social networks
       (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram).


   Name of the Cookie uvc
   Provider           AddtoAny
   Purpose            Sharing on social media. For more information


   These services do not deposit any cookies. Cookies are not the only
   technology to store information about you, a service that does not use
   cookies can store information via: flash cookies, directly on its
   server using a fingerprint, using html5 storage…

100 dessins de Cartooning pour la Liberté de la Presse

   Reporters sans frontières publie son 44ème album : « 100 dessins de
   Cartooning for Peacepour la liberté de la presse »!

   Le 5 décembre 2013, Reporters sans frontières publie un nouvel album
   entièrement dédié aux dessinateurs de presse : 100 dessins de
   Cartooning for Peace pour la liberté de la presse. Plus de cinquante
   auteurs du monde entier ont collaboré à cet ouvrage en traitant les
   thèmes de la liberté d’expression, de l’état du monde et d’un sujet
   d’une actualité brûlante : « le monde sur écoute ».

   Maintenant disponible en version digitale sur l’Apple Store pour 7,99€
   !
