Offre de formation

   Les établissements de l’Université Clermont Auvergne & associés
   couvrent un large éventail de formations, aux niveaux DU, DUT, Licence,
   Master et Doctorat qui s’inscrivent dans le processus de Bologne.

   Des cursus d’ingénieur sont également proposés dans les écoles et
   disciplines suivantes : VetAgroSup (agronomie, sciences des aliments et
   développement territorial), IFMA (mécanique et génie industriel),
   Polytech’ (génie biologique, génie civil, génie électrique, génie
   mathématique et génie physique), ISIMA (informatique) et ENSCCF (chimie
   et génie chimique).

   L’originalité et l’excellence de nos formations se traduisent par
   l’intégration dans tous nos cursus d’une dimension à la fois
   professionnelle et scientifique grâce aux nombreux partenariats noués
   avec des entreprises nationales et internationales, et également aux
   nombreux projets de recherche menés dans les 53 laboratoires
   universitaires auxquels sont adossées l’ensemble des formations
   proposées et dont plusieurs sont reconnus comme laboratoires
   d’excellence.

EURAXESS Auvergne - English page

   More information : http://ec.europa.eu/euraxess/

   The objective of the Centre de Services Euraxess Auvergne (CSEA) is to
   offer support for overseas researchers visiting Auvergne in facilities
   linked to the Université Clermont-Auvergne and its associate
   institutions.

   The CSEA is a member of the European Euraxess network which currently
   includes more than 200 centres in 37 different countries. EURAXESS is a
   European Commission initiative designed to facilitate the mobility of
   researchers in Europe and boost the appeal of the European Research
   Area (ERA).

   The Centre de Services Euraxess Auvergne of Université Clermont
   Auvergne et Associés is the regional branch of the EURAXESS network and
   the reference point for all administrative procedures necessary when
   hosting overseas researchers.

   The CSEA has been operational since January 2009 and has offered
   support to around 100 researchers from more than 20 countries, within
   the 12 participating institutions.
     * Who should contact the Centre de Services Euraxess Auvergne ?
     * What services does the CSEA provides ?
     * Who to contact ?
     * Where to find us ?

Who should contact the Centre de Services EURAXESS Auvergne ?

   The CSEA is the first point of contact for visiting doctoral
   researchers, post-docs, researchers and teaching staff from overseas in
   Auvergne. It also provides assistance for their hosting researchers in
   anticipating and preparing for the arrival of foreign colleagues at
   their research centres.

   The CSEA is particularly intended to help research centres that are
   part of the Université Clermont-Auvergne and its associate institutions
   l’Université Clermont-Auvergne et Associés.

   -   If you are a researcher visiting from overseas

   The CSEA offers you and your family a personalised welcome and support.

   It can also help prepare your visit before you arrive. So please get in
   touch directly with the CSEA and we will assist you with your
   preparatory procedures.

   -   If you are hosting an overseas researcher

   The CSEA is on hand to answer any information queries and assist you
   with the procedures required before the arrival of your overseas
   colleagues. So please get in touch with the CSEA before they arrive.

What services does the CSEA provides ?

   International researchers can benefit from the support of the CSEA in
   the following areas (this list is not exhaustive) :
     * formalities for admission and residency on French soil :
       information about hosting agreements, procedures for visas and
       residency permits, etc.

     * looking for accomodation : personnalised offers (apartments, hotel
       résidences, university studios, etc.) and lists of contacts.

     * insurance procedures : information about social security,
       additional health coverage, workplace accident insurance, and
       housing insurance.

     * practical info : schooling, childcare, French classes in
       Clermont-Ferrand, opening a bank account in France, etc.

   Don’t hesitate to download our brochure !

   PDF - 2.6 Mo

Who to contact ?

   You can contact CSEA : Samira RIAD, Emmeline BLANC
   Tel : 04 73 40 63 21 / 63 29
   Email : euraxess-auvergne@uca.fr

Where to find us ?

   The Centre de Services EURAXESS Auvergne is located in the
   International Relations Department, Villa Blatin, 36 bis Boulevard Côte
   Blatin, 63 000 Clermont-Ferrand, France.

EURAXESS Auvergne - English page

   More information : http://ec.europa.eu/euraxess/

   The objective of the Centre de Services Euraxess Auvergne (CSEA) is to
   offer support for overseas researchers visiting Auvergne in facilities
   linked to the Université Clermont-Auvergne and its associate
   institutions.

   The CSEA is a member of the European Euraxess network which currently
   includes more than 200 centres in 37 different countries. EURAXESS is a
   European Commission initiative designed to facilitate the mobility of
   researchers in Europe and boost the appeal of the European Research
   Area (ERA).

   The Centre de Services Euraxess Auvergne of Université Clermont
   Auvergne et Associés is the regional branch of the EURAXESS network and
   the reference point for all administrative procedures necessary when
   hosting overseas researchers.

   The CSEA has been operational since January 2009 and has offered
   support to around 100 researchers from more than 20 countries, within
   the 12 participating institutions.
     * Who should contact the Centre de Services Euraxess Auvergne ?
     * What services does the CSEA provides ?
     * Who to contact ?
     * Where to find us ?

Who should contact the Centre de Services EURAXESS Auvergne ?

   The CSEA is the first point of contact for visiting doctoral
   researchers, post-docs, researchers and teaching staff from overseas in
   Auvergne. It also provides assistance for their hosting researchers in
   anticipating and preparing for the arrival of foreign colleagues at
   their research centres.

   The CSEA is particularly intended to help research centres that are
   part of the Université Clermont-Auvergne and its associate institutions
   l’Université Clermont-Auvergne et Associés.

   -   If you are a researcher visiting from overseas

   The CSEA offers you and your family a personalised welcome and support.

   It can also help prepare your visit before you arrive. So please get in
   touch directly with the CSEA and we will assist you with your
   preparatory procedures.

   -   If you are hosting an overseas researcher

   The CSEA is on hand to answer any information queries and assist you
   with the procedures required before the arrival of your overseas
   colleagues. So please get in touch with the CSEA before they arrive.

What services does the CSEA provides ?

   International researchers can benefit from the support of the CSEA in
   the following areas (this list is not exhaustive) :
     * formalities for admission and residency on French soil :
       information about hosting agreements, procedures for visas and
       residency permits, etc.

     * looking for accomodation : personnalised offers (apartments, hotel
       résidences, university studios, etc.) and lists of contacts.

     * insurance procedures : information about social security,
       additional health coverage, workplace accident insurance, and
       housing insurance.

     * practical info : schooling, childcare, French classes in
       Clermont-Ferrand, opening a bank account in France, etc.

   Don’t hesitate to download our brochure !

Where to find us ?

   Blatin, 63 000 Clermont-Ferrand, France.
