                       Behind the scenes at Groupe ADP

Take flight at the Group's head office

   As part of the European Heritage Days event, Groupe ADP is opening the
   doors of its head office to the public on Saturday 21st September, from
   10h00 to 18h00. Young and old alike will be free to wander around the
   building, with access to the atrium, the Innovation Hub area and the
   terrace, boasting a view of Concorde and the Paris-Charles de Gaulle
   Airport taxiways. In addition, there will be a host of surprises for
   visitors... ask us for the full programme!

   This constitutes a first for Groupe ADP, which has decided to open the
   doors of its head office to welcome the public as part of the 36th
   edition of the European Heritage Days initiative. The new head office
   was inaugurated in March 2017, and is located at the heart of
   Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport. As well as providing an open window
   onto the airport, this project reflects the global ambition of the
   group. Visitors on the day will be able to enjoy an immersive
   experience as they try out a flight simulator or aircraft marshalling
   simulator. They can also get a sneak peek at the new business workwear,
   watch three films about the Group and visit the Innovation Hub, which
   will show off its new home equipped with an amazing giant screen. The
   highlight of the show: a chance to visit the building's terrace to
   admire the ballet-like movements of the aircraft at Paris-Charles de
   Gaulle, and the Air France Concorde on display at the edge of the
   taxiways. Lastly, after enjoying ice cream and candy floss, you can
   record your visit for posterity in the form of a souvenir photo to take
   home!

   Head Office : 1, rue de France 93290 Tremblay-en-France
   Opening Hours : 10h00 to 18h00
   Parking : Free parking near to the head office.

airport.

Take flight at the Group's head office

   As part of the European Heritage Days event, Groupe ADP is opening the
   doors of its head office to the public on Saturday 21st September, from
   10h00 to 18h00. Young and old alike will be free to wander around the
   building, with access to the atrium, the Innovation Hub area and the
   terrace, boasting a view of Concorde and the Paris-Charles de Gaulle
   Airport taxiways. In addition, there will be a host of surprises for
   visitors... ask us for the full programme!

   This constitutes a first for Groupe ADP, which has decided to open the
   doors of its head office to welcome the public as part of the 36th
   edition of the European Heritage Days initiative. The new head office
   was inaugurated in March 2017, and is located at the heart of
   Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport. As well as providing an open window
   onto the airport, this project reflects the global ambition of the
   group. Visitors on the day will be able to enjoy an immersive
   experience as they try out a flight simulator or aircraft marshalling
   simulator. They can also get a sneak peek at the new business workwear,
   watch three films about the Group and visit the Innovation Hub, which
   will show off its new home equipped with an amazing giant screen. The
   highlight of the show: a chance to visit the building's terrace to
   admire the ballet-like movements of the aircraft at Paris-Charles de
   Gaulle, and the Air France Concorde on display at the edge of the
   taxiways. Lastly, after enjoying ice cream and candy floss, you can
   record your visit for posterity in the form of a souvenir photo to take
   home!
