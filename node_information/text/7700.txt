   What’s New about Sleep?

What’s New about Sleep?

   Sleep well for a fuller everyday life

   Even though the exact roles of sleep still elude us, we are learning
   more and more about its effects and influences. Energy metabolism,
   immunity, cellular activity, cognitive performance, mental health...
   Current scientific findings reveal that each of these functions
   benefits from sleep. The product of a collaboration among sleep
   specialists and a journalist, this book discusses the virtues of sleep
   and how it contributes to health and well-being. It also examines the
   potential negative effects of inadequate sleep.

   Flora of Ile-de-France

Flora of Ile-de-France

   What do we know about the infinite floristic diversity and wealth of
   the Ile-de-France region?
   Extending far beyond received ideas trying to make the capital bled dry
   of any natural component, this unprecedented scientific basis for our
   regional flora was long overdue.
   After fifty years of expeditions to collect samples, meticulous
   verification in herbariums, a literature survey and validation of a
   colossal amount of collected data, the two authors present their
   findings in two volumes. No less than 1619 species are thus identified
   and linked to some 1500 distribution maps.
   This complete work, of exceptional scientific quality yet still
   attractive and accessible, a reference assessment of the Ile-de-France
   flora thanks to an unrivalled regional floristic database, will enthral
   a wide audience.
   Access the publication
