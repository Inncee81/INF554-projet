Welcome to data.bnf.fr.

   The Bibliothèque nationale de France guides you through its resources
   by aggregating information scattered among its various catalogues and
   the Galica digital library on dedicated HTML pages.

   Data.bnf.fr is an open data project based on semantic web standards and
   tools.

   In 2013, data.bnf.fr and Gallica won the Stanford Prize for Innovation
   in Research Libraries (SPIRL).
