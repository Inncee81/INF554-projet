   (BUTTON) (BUTTON)
     * Introducing Collections
    Introducing Collections
       Collections are an accessible way to manage your icons library, and
       the core of our web app. Do you want to know how to use them? Press
       the right arrow or use the top navigation to watch the guide.
     * Add icons
    Add icons
       Browse the packs or search icons and add them to your active
       collection with this button.
     * List view
    List view
       This is a list of your collections. You can change their names, and
       they are sorted by use. Click on one of them to start using it.
     * Create a collection
    Create a collection
       Click on this button to create a new collection.
     * Active collection
    Active collection
       This is your active collection. A collection can have up to 256
       icons if you are a registered user or 50 if you are not registered.
     * Collection options
    Collection options
       Access this menu to edit your collection; edit your icons
       separately, duplicate your collection, sort the icons in your
       collection or share it with the rest of the world.
     * SVG Upload
    SVG Upload
       Drag & drop your SVGs to the active collection to upload your own
       icons. There is a limitation of 3 icons per collection for free
       users.
     * Fullscreen
    Fullscreen
       Not enough space? Now you can see your collections in fullscreen
       mode to make it easier for you.
     * Delete
    Delete
       Are you done with this collection? Delete it and create a new one
       here.
     * Enjoy!
    Enjoy!
       And that is all. If you have any questions, you can contact our
       support team here.
       Thank you for using Flaticon, we hope you enjoy our service

                                   Icon fonts

   Icon fonts let you to quickly convert vector icons into webfonts. This
   means you can display vector icons in your website and control them
   with CSS. Check this page to learn more about icon fonts and how to use
   them with Flaticon.

Features

     * 1. High resolution
       Icons are scalable and retina-ready. Perfect for a responsive
       design. They look nice on screens of any resolution.
     * 2. Quick and easy
       Building iconfonts with Flaticon is easier than ever. Add icons to
       your collections and download them in seconds.
     * 3. Total control CSS
       Iconfonts are basically text, so you can change their aspect easily
       with CSS.
     * 4. Accessible
       Flaticon name symbols with common terms to help screen readers
       understand their meaning ;-).
     * 5. Animatable
       CSS transitions and animations are completely compatible with
       iconfonts.
     * 6. Tidy
       No more loads of images or cumbersome sprites. Stop wasting your
       time!

   class="flaticon-heart"></i> icons</p>

How to use icon font with flaticon

   Iconfonts Tutorial Part 1

1. Prepare your collection

   Add monocolor icons to the collection for your website

2. Download iconfont

   Open your collection and press the Download collection button, then
   select Iconfont
   Iconfonts Tutorial Part 2

   Iconfonts Tutorial Part 3

3. Prepare your collection

   Copy the source files and CSS to your web folder.

4. Load the stylesheet

   Request the CSS stylesheet from the head of your web.
   <link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css"
   href="your_website_domain/css_root/flaticon.css">
   Iconfonts Tutorial Part 4

   Iconfonts Tutorial Part 5

5. Use the classes

   Use the classes of each icon and they will appear on your website.
   After, manipulate them with CSS.
   Use example: <i class="flaticon-airplane49"></i> or <span
   class="flaticon-airplane49"></span>
