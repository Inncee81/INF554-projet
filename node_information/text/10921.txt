Lo que hacemos









   Welcome to Power Hiking Paris , registered law 1901 non profit hiking
   organisation and affiliated to the Fédération Française de la Randonnée
   Pédestre
   (https://www.ffrandonnee.fr/clubs-et-comites/clubs/10099/power-hiking-p
   aris.aspx) since 2016.
   We are a friendly group of international hikers living in the Paris
   area. Our hikes are mostly hosted in English. We walk an average of
   22-30km, every Sunday, in Ile de France at a brisk pace (5 to 7 km/h).
   Our walks are rated as below :
   - Newcomers (5-5.5 km/h)
   - Regular (5.5 - 6.5 km/h)
   - Challenging (6 - 7 km/h)
   You need to be fit enough if you want to take part (otherwise you might
   not be able to keep up). BEAR IN MIND THAT POWER HIKING IS A WORKOUT
   ACTIVITY (NOT SIGHTSEEING) AND THEREFORE NOT SUITABLE FOR EVERYONE.
   All our hikes are led by trained volunteers hike leaders.
   Over the past 4 years, we have organised over 470 events comprising Day
   hikes, WE away * and Hiking Adventure * in other France's neighbourhood
   (Queyras July'17, Vanoise July'19) or abroad (Scotland July '16,
   Vietnam November' 16, Portugal May '17, Bulgaria August' 17, Sri Lanka
   November '17, Madeira March'18, Madagascar November' 18, Bulgaria
   Snowshoeing March'19, Ireland July'19) . Thousand of hikers have walked
   and traveled with us. That makes Power Hiking Paris the most
   successful, the most active and also the most affordable (3€ fees/walk)
   Hiking Group on Meetup in the Paris Region.
   The Group Motto : "Alone we go faster, together we go further" –
   African wisdom.
   Last but not least, this community is also about building bonds of
   friendship between the members. Respectfulness, Courteousness and
   Friendliness are our values.

グループ概要









   Welcome to Power Hiking Paris , registered law 1901 non profit hiking
   organisation and affiliated to the Fédération Française de la Randonnée
   Pédestre
   (https://www.ffrandonnee.fr/clubs-et-comites/clubs/10099/power-hiking-p
   aris.aspx) since 2016.
   We are a friendly group of international hikers living in the Paris
   area. Our hikes are mostly hosted in English. We walk an average of
   22-30km, every Sunday, in Ile de France at a brisk pace (5 to 7 km/h).
   Our walks are rated as below :
   - Newcomers (5-5.5 km/h)
   - Regular (5.5 - 6.5 km/h)
   - Challenging (6 - 7 km/h)
   You need to be fit enough if you want to take part (otherwise you might
   not be able to keep up). BEAR IN MIND THAT POWER HIKING IS A WORKOUT
   ACTIVITY (NOT SIGHTSEEING) AND THEREFORE NOT SUITABLE FOR EVERYONE.
   All our hikes are led by trained volunteers hike leaders.
   Over the past 4 years, we have organised over 470 events comprising Day
   hikes, WE away * and Hiking Adventure * in other France's neighbourhood
   (Queyras July'17, Vanoise July'19) or abroad (Scotland July '16,
   Vietnam November' 16, Portugal May '17, Bulgaria August' 17, Sri Lanka
   November '17, Madeira March'18, Madagascar November' 18, Bulgaria
   Snowshoeing March'19, Ireland July'19) . Thousand of hikers have walked
   and traveled with us. That makes Power Hiking Paris the most
   successful, the most active and also the most affordable (3€ fees/walk)
   Hiking Group on Meetup in the Paris Region.
   The Group Motto : "Alone we go faster, together we go further" –
   African wisdom.
   Last but not least, this community is also about building bonds of
   friendship between the members. Respectfulness, Courteousness and
   Friendliness are our values.
   Before you sign up for this group, please read our Terms and Conditions
   (https://app.box.com/s/h2tvywioglu7ia5f5rwvwaf74cy7ef4w). Being member
   of Power Hiking Paris means you fully understood and agreed with those
   and we are very strict on expecting members to abide by the rules.
   If you are looking for getting outdoors with active and friendly
   people. Sign up now. Become a Power Hiker.
