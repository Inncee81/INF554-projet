   A place to share beautiful images of interior design, residential

   Expect a variety of pictures that please and capture the imagination.

   GeorgianaAesthetic.tumblr, if you're looking for me.

   My sincere thanks to the talented design professionals whose work is
   posted on the blog and to those who have sent very kind notes and

   please let me know and I will do so immediately.
